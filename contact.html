QUICK ANSWERS

When does my order ship?

Drops ship in the order they’re placed once a batch is ready. You’ll get a tracking email the moment yours is on the way. Need a status check? Call or text the number up top — fastest way.

I have a food allergy — are these safe for me?

Every jar lists its full ingredients and allergens on its product page under What’s Inside. Always read those before ordering. If you’re unsure, text us before you buy — we’d rather answer than have you guess.

Something’s wrong with my order.

We’ve got you. Text your order number to the line above or use the message form and we’ll make it right — that’s the whole “love it or your money back” deal.

Can I suggest a flavor?

Please do — that’s literally how we pick what’s next. Drop it in Request a Revival above. Real submissions have become real jars.
