FREQUENTLY ASKED

When do pre-orders ship?

Pre-orders ship out a couple of weeks after they're placed. You'll get a tracking email the second yours is on the way.

Do my Nostalgic Desserts need to be refrigerated?

Unless otherwise noted on the label or cap, 95% of our flavors are shelf-stable and do NOT need to be refrigerated!

Are they safe for my allergies?

Every flavor's full ingredients and allergens are listed right on its product page under “What's Inside.” Check there before ordering — and reach out if you're ever unsure.

Why are some Nostalgic Desserts layered?

This is a very good question. To truly capture a flavor — let's say Ice Cream Truck Strawberry Shortcake — you can't just have a base nut butter with a bunch of different flavorings mixed in, then throw toppings on the base and expect it to taste authentic. You won't get the true taste of the flavor you're trying to replicate. Sure, it's easier to do it that way, but creating perfection isn't easy — which is why we truly mimic the flavor by using the layers.

Do you ship internationally?

Unfortunately we do not at the moment. Sorry about that!

Is the 35 lb bucket really worth it?

If you go through a lot of one flavor, absolutely — it's 35 one-pound jars in a single bucket for $399, well under buying them one at a time, and shipping's included.

What's the free-shipping threshold?

Orders of $65 or more ship free. (The 35 lb bucket always ships free.)

Returns & money-back guarantee?

Every order is backed by our 30-day money-back guarantee. Not happy with it? We'll make it right.
